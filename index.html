What is WBTC Bridge?

WBTC Network is the official Wrapped Bitcoin destination for an ERC-20 tokenized BTC asset on Ethereum; verify the canonical contract on Etherscan before connecting a wallet.

How WBTC Bridge works

  1. Verify the destinationOpen the official WBTC domain and compare the token contract before using any wallet prompt or third-party route.
  2. Choose the routeFor BTC to WBTC or redemption, follow the current supported merchant, exchange, or app path shown from the official destination.
  3. Review assumptionsConfirm network, recipient address, custody model, confirmations, and any provider fees before submitting a transaction.
  4. Track settlementUse Bitcoin and Ethereum explorers to confirm the BTC leg, WBTC mint, burn, or ERC-20 transfer status.

Before you bridge

BTC representation on Ethereum

WBTC is an ERC-20 representation of Bitcoin used across Ethereum wallets, exchanges, and DeFi apps; the canonical contract is visible on Etherscan.

Mint and burn framework

The WBTC model uses custodians and merchants for minting and burning, as described in the Wrapped Tokens whitepaper.

Bridge risk matters

Cross-chain movement can introduce smart-contract, operator, custody, and user-error risks; Ethereum.org explains the main bridge trade-offs.

WBTC Bridge FAQ

What is a wbtc bridge used for?

A wbtc bridge route is used to move between native BTC exposure and WBTC on Ethereum, or to move WBTC through supported cross-chain paths when available.

Is wbtc bridge the same as wrapping Bitcoin?

Not exactly. Wrapping BTC into WBTC uses a custodian and merchant model, while general bridging can also mean moving an already wrapped token across blockchain networks.

What should I verify before using a wbtc bridge?

Verify the official domain, token contract, connected network, recipient address, provider terms, and bridge risks before approving any transaction.

Does the wbtc bridge have guaranteed fees or speed?

No. Fees and timing depend on Bitcoin confirmations, Ethereum gas, the selected provider, and any merchant or exchange process; avoid pages claiming fixed returns or guaranteed settlement.

Where is the official wbtc bridge app?

Use the official WBTC destination at wbtc.network and follow the current app or merchant route shown there; do not trust lookalike domains.
